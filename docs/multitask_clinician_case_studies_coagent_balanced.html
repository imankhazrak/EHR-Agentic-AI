Scope and selection intent

  • Tasks: lipid_next, diabetes_current, hypertension_current, obesity_current, cardio_next, kidney_next, stroke_next.
  • Balanced target set: 1 TP, 1 TN, 1 FP, 1 FN per task (28 cases total).
  • Confidence ranking rule: maximize abs(probability - 0.5) within each bucket.

Cross-task clinician checklist

  • Verify temporal framing (*_current vs *_next) before actioning risk.
  • Reconcile diagnosis, procedure, and medication evidence against model rationale.
  • For FP predictions, check for confounding due to prophylaxis or history-only documentation.
  • For FN predictions, check hidden severity cues and recurrent disease trajectories.
  • Review cardio-renal-metabolic-neuro overlap before final adjudication.
